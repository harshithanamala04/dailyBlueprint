Standard Weight Classifications

BMI values are organized according to World Health Organization (WHO) protocols:

BMI RANGE CLASSIFICATION HEALTH RISK
Below 18.5 ● Underweight Minimal to Moderate
18.5 – 24.9 ● Normal (Healthy) Very Low
25.0 – 29.9 ● Overweight Increased
30.0 and Above ● Obese High / Severe
📏 Quick Conversion Reference
  • Feet to Inches: Multiply feet by 12 and add the remaining inches (e.g., 5'4" = 60 + 4 = 64 inches).
  • cm to Meters: Shift the decimal point two spaces left (e.g., 175 cm = 1.75 m).
  • kg to lbs: Multiply kilograms by 2.205.
